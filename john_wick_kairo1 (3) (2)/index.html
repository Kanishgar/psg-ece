Department of ECE

The Department of ECE at PSG College of Technology is dedicated to imparting world-class education and fostering innovation in the field of computing.

PSG College of Technology

Established in 1951, PSG College of Technology is a premier institution in India, renowned for its academic excellence and industry collaborations. With a strong focus on innovation and practical learning, the college empowers students to excel in their respective fields.

The college's vibrant campus is home to students from diverse backgrounds, making it a hub for cultural and intellectual exchange.
